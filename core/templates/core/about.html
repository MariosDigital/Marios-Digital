About Us

Heirs of an Electronic Dynasty

A Tradition of Excellence

In our company, we uphold a family tradition that dates back to the beginnings of my father, Mr. Mario Montoya Córdoba, in the city of Veracruz, Mexico. Over a decade ago, we decided to continue his legacy and founded our own electronic equipment repair company, maintaining the same passion for innovation and excellence that he instilled in us.

The Legacy of Mario Montoya Córdoba: My father, Mario, was a visionary in the field of electronics. From his early days in the business, he stood out for his ability to diagnose and solve problems in electronic devices efficiently. His dedication and love for technology are qualities that still inspire our daily work.
